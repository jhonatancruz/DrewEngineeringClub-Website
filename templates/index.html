About

HackDrew is a 10-hour hackathon at Drew University, hosted by the Drew Engineering Club. We welcome Drew students to join us in building awesome software and hardware projects.

We encourage beginner and advanced hackers alike to challenge themselves and expand their skills. We'll have free food, swag, workshops, mentorship, prizes, games and more.

FAQs

What is a Hackathon?

HackDrew is a 10-hour hackathon at Drew University, hosted by the Drew Engineering Club. We welcome Drew students to join us in building awesome software and hardware projects.

What should I bring?

Yourself, your laptop, and your creativity!

Where is it?

HackDrew will be located in LC-30 and LC-28 this year! If you need help getting there, shoot us an email.

What if I've never been to a Hackathon?

We encourage beginner and advanced hackers alike to challenge themselves and expand their skills. We'll have free food, swag, workshops, mentorship, prizes, games and more.

Who can come?

Any Drew student that has an idea and is interested in creating an application or game with either hardware or software.
